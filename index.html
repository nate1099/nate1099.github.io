What I Learned

This class was an absolute enjoyment to me. There are few other classes that I would be willing to get up at 6 AM for. In this class, I learned how to use Photoshop in a way that I haven't been able to before. My family this past summer moved to South Carolina. With the photoshopping skills that I learned in this class, I was able to photoshop myself into some of the images that they have taken. It isn't the same as being there in person, but it's the closest that I can currently get.

On top of Photoshop, I also learned how to use Illustrator. As I am someone that owns a Cricut machine, I will be able to use this moving forward to make whatever projects my heart desires.

I also learned how to use InDesign, which can use a culmination of all of the knowledge I obtained and use it collectively to create flyers for any events I may have in the future.

I also learned helpful concepts on design priciples that I have already used in other applications, such as alignment, contrast, and balance.

Additionally, and most importantly, this class has shown me just how much I love doing graphic design. While I am graduating this semester, I think I will continue to find ways to further my education and knowledge in the field of graphic design.
