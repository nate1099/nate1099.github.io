Project Description

I believe that what and who we are is most fruitfully reflected in what, who and how we love. Because of this concept my self-portrait intentionally carries a theme of all the people/places/things that I love the most. The image begins with an empty block of my silhouette. As I would be nothing without all the people and passions that define me. The sunrise as the background is a reminder that each day is a new beginning. Make the most of each one, and remember that the next day is another chance for you to do what you haven't done yet.

Make the most of today,
before it becomes yesterday.
